How to play the lineups and result guesser.

First you select which match you want to guess on by selecting opponent.
Then you fill in the score and match events and select the lineup.
When youÂ´re finished, press the confirm button.

How the scoring works.

  • Liverpool goals: 5pts
  • Opponent goals: 5pts
  • Minute of first goal: Exact: 20pts, +-5min: 10pts, +- 10min: 2pts
  • Number of yellows: Exact: 10pts, +-2: 5pts
  • First Goalscorer: 20pts
  • Red card: Correct: 10pts, Incorrect: -10pts
  • Lineup: 2pts per correct player, 30pts for correct lineup
